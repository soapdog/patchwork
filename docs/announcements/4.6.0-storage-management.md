# v4.6.0: Storage Management

I added a new tool to help you manage the amount of space used by your blobs. You can find it under the `MORE` button, it is called `Storage Manager`.

It lets you quickly view how much disk space your blobs are using and quickly remove blobs by either last access date or size.

There's no automatic removal or constant pruning of the blobs folder. It is a manual process, whenever you feel like your blobs are using too much disk space, you can use that tool to free that space up.
